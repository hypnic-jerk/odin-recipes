Pasta Carbonara

Photo by Pinar Kucuk on Unsplash

Description

Carbonara is one of the most popular and loved dishes of the Italian cuisine, it has a rich taste and a pleasant texture which makes it a popular choice for many.

What you'll need:

  • Olive oil - 2 tsp
  • Guanciale (cured pork cheek) - 1 lbs
  • Spaghetti - 1 (16oz) package
  • Eggs - 3 (large)
  • Pecorino Romano cheese - 10 tbsp
  • Salt and pepper - to taste

How to make it:

  1. Step 1

    Gather all the ingredients.

  2. Step 2
    • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat; add guanciale.
    • Cook, turning occasionally, until evenly browned and crispy (5 to 10 minutes).
    • Remove from heat and drain on paper towels.
  3. Step 3
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bite (about 9 minutes).
    • Drain and return to the pot. Let cool, stirring occasionally (about 5 minutes).
  4. Step 4
    • Whisk eggs, 1/2 of the Pecorino Romano cheese, and some black pepper in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
  5. Step 5
    • Pour egg mixture over pasta, stirring quickly, until creamy and slightly cooled.
    • Stir in guanciale.
  6. Step 5
    • Top with remaining Pecorino Romano cheese and more black pepper.
  7. Step 5

    8. Serve and enjoy!
